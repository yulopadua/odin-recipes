Together Breakfast inspired by Steven Universe

Description

Waffles topped with whipped cream, maple syrup, popcorn and fresh strawberries in this Steven Universe Together Breakfast!

This is the basic recipe. Check out this recipe if you want the gourmet version!

Ingredients

  • 4 toaster waffles, toasted
  • As needed maple syrup
  • 1 package microwave popcorn
  • As needed canned whipped cream
  • 1 fresh strawberry

Steps

  1. Stack the waffles on a plate.
  2. Top with maple syrup, popcorn, whipped cream, and strawberry.
  3. Enjoy.
